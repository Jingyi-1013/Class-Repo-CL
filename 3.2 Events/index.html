"Raise the Red Lantern" is a multimedia theater show inspired by Zhang Yimou's film "Raise the Red Lantern". This piece explores the connections and parallels in women's lives across different eras, examining their pursuit of art, careers, family, and love, while unraveling how their fates intertwine and diverge over time. The performance blends elements of Chinese traditional arts, language barriers, and dance fusion.
